About

I'm Alec Dewulf, and I'm currently a Junior at Ashbury College in Ottawa, Ontario. I have very strong interests in competitive programming, machine learning, and technology which I hope will provide the grounds for a career in computer science. This goal of this website is to share some of my thoughts and ideas via blogging. If you would like to connect and possibly work together, please contact me at alec@cisudbury.ca.
